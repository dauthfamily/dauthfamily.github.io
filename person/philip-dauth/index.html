Philip Dauth

Phillip Dauth was born in Westhofen, Germany in September 1866 to Andreas and Elizabeth Dauth. In 1882, at the age of 16, he immigrated to the United States to join his siblings living in Colorado. He first made a visit to his eldest brother Louis in Fort Collins before deciding to settle in Denver near his sister Caroline (Lena) and her family.

In 1883 Phillip began a wire working apprenticeship at the Denver Iron Fence Company, a business half owned by Lena's husband, Jacob Fitting. Once certified as a wire worker Phillip worked full time for the company. In 1886 he made a short move to Fort Collins to sell wire flower stands and work with his two brothers, Louis and George, in their grocery businesses.

On June 2nd 1887 Phillip married Katie Hornung. They had their first daughter Amelia “Isabella” on August 31st 1889. By 1890 Phillip had decided he wanted to run his own business, and partnered with R.W. Bailey and P.C. Brueggemann to incorporate the Rocky Mountain Wire & Iron Works. After a year Philip decided he wanted to try the grocery business, and partnered with E. Griffel to create the Griffel & Dauth grocery.

Phillips had his second daughter, Louise, in January 1893 and first son, Philip Jr. in November 1895. In 1896 Phillip returned to wire working roots with the Denver Wire & Iron Works Company. Phillip’s second son Fredrick “Fred” was born February 1897, and youngest daughter Elizabeth “Elsie” was born in December 1898.

Philip had begun experiencing health problems around 1890, which physicians determined to be symptoms of Bright's Disease. As his health continued to fail he was told by doctors that he should quit working and focus on his well being, and so he moved his family to a small farm in Vineland, Colorado near Pueblo. In 1905 Katie unexpectedly passed away. Phillip burdened by the loss of his wife, the chronic pain of his illness, and trying to care for five children, took his own life that same year. Philip died at the age of 39. He is buried at Roselawn Cemetery in Pueblo, Colorado next to Katie.
