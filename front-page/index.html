Welcome to the Dauth Family Archive. This site is a digital archive and genealogical database for decedents and relatives of Andreas Dauth, who lived in the small German village of Westhofen during the 1800s. Andreas and Elisabeth had all together ten children, one of whom was my great grandfather, George Dauth. Many of the stories, photos, and documents found here are related to his experiences and those of his descendants.

The Dauth Family Archive aims at providing an account of Dauth Family History.

For more information get in contact with Nathaniel Dauth at https://nathanieldauth.github.io/
